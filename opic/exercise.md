
### 1. Tell me about exercise you do. Do you jog? Do you work out with weights? Or do you go for a walk from time to time? Also, tell me how often you exercise and for how long.

최근엔 날씨가 추워서 조깅을 하고 있지 않습니다. 날이 따뜻했을땐 일주일에 세번 조깅을 하였습니다. 집앞에 있는 강변을 30분정도 달렸습니다. 주로 저녁시간대에 이어폰으로 음악을 들으며 혼자 달립니다. 건강유지와 스트레스 해소가 목적입니다. 다시 날이 따뜻해지면 조깅을 시작할 계획입니다.

```
These days, I don’t go jogging because the weather is too cold.
However, when the weather was warmer, I used to jog about three times a week.

I usually ran along the riverside near my house for around 30 minutes.
Most of the time, I went jogging in the evening, listening to music with my earphones, and I preferred to run alone.

The main reason I jog is to stay healthy and relieve stress.
Once the weather gets warmer again, I’m planning to start jogging regularly.

```

